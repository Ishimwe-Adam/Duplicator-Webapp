Based on the product lines typically associated with Duplicator Ltd (and its primary partners like Duplo International), here is a structured breakdown of their product categories.

The company specializes in high-speed digital duplication and precision print finishing, moving beyond standard office copiers into industrial-grade production.

📂 Digital Duplicators
These are the core products designed for high-volume, low-cost printing. They bridge the gap between a standard photocopier and an offset press.

Entry-Level Models: Compact systems like the DP-A200, designed for small offices or schools needing fast, affordable duplication.  

Mid-Range Systems: Models like the DP-G300, offering higher resolution and increased speed for medium-sized organizations.  

High-Volume Production: Heavy-duty machines like the DP-X850, capable of speeds up to 200 pages per minute with 600 dpi quality, built for long, continuous print runs.  

📂 Finishing & Cutting Solutions
Automation tools used to turn raw printed sheets into final products like cards, menus, or brochures.  

Slitters, Cutters, & Creasers: The DC-Range (e.g., DC-618, DC-646). These machines perform multiple tasks in one pass—slitting, cutting, and creasing—to eliminate manual labor.  

Digital Cutting Tables: The PFi Blade series, used for short-run packaging, custom-shaped labels, and prototypes without the need for physical dies.  

Die-Cutters: Precision machines like the DSM-1000 for high-end, bespoke packaging and card production.  

📂 Booklet Makers & Binding
Systems that automate the assembly of multi-page documents.

Booklet Making Systems: Ranges like the DBM-150 and DBM-700. These include features for saddle stitching, folding, and creating square spines for a "perfect bound" look.  

Binding Machines: Automated thermal and glue binding systems (like the DPB-500) for creating professional soft-cover books and manuals.  

Folding Systems: High-speed machines for complex document folds used in direct mail and marketing materials.  

📂 Print Embellishment
Technology used to add "luxury" textures and visual effects to printed materials.

Sensory Effects: The DuSense range, which adds raised UV spot coating (3D textures) to business cards, book covers, and high-end invitations.

📂 Consumables & Support
Inks & Masters: Specific high-yield ink cartridges and master rolls required for digital duplicator operation.

Toner & Parts: Replacement components and toner for integrated copier/printer systems.

Software: Workflow and connectivity tools (like Ultimate Impostrip) to automate the layout and prepress process.
